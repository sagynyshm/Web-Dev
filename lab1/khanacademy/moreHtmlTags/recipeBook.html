Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Bacon 200g
Eggs 4
Parmesan cheese 100g
Black pepper To taste

Steps:

  • Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  • Fry the bacon in a pan until crispy, then chop into small pieces.
  • Whisk the eggs and mix with Parmesan cheese and black pepper.
  • Combine the hot pasta, bacon, and egg mixture, mixing quickly.
  • Serve with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.

Chicken Alfredo

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 2 breasts
Fettuccine pasta 400g
Heavy cream 1 cup
Butter 2 tbsp
Garlic 3 cloves
Parmesan cheese 100g
Salt and pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook in a skillet until golden brown.
  3. Remove the chicken and slice it into strips.
  4. In the same skillet, melt butter and cook garlic until fragrant. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until smooth, then add the pasta and chicken.
  6. Serve with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.
